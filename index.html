About me

I'm a passionate Java developer with 3+ years of experience building high-performance web applications. By day, I'm wrangling code with Java EE, Spring Boot, Hibernate, Thymeleaf, and AWS, but when I'm not, you might find me curled up with a good book or putting pen to paper myself (because hey, a developer's gotta unwind!).

Whether it's tackling a new coding challenge or exploring a new story, I believe in living a balanced life that fuels both my professional and personal passions.
